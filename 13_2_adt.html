Los Biomas

Biomes of the world

The climate and topography are of great importance in the formation of biomes.

For example, some mountain ranges with enormous interposed walls in the path of the humid winds coming from the ocean.

When they collide with the mountain, these winds release their moisture on the slope facing the sea, while the other slope is arid.

Likewise, height is a determining factor: in the mountains, biomes are distributed in zones according to altitude.
